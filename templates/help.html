Help - Field Description

  • Gender : Select the gender of applicant: Male or Female.
  • Married : Choose Yes if the applicant is married.
  • Dependents : Number of people dependent on the applicant.
  • Education : Select Graduate or Not Graduate.
  • Self Employed : Select Yes if the applicant runs their own business or works independently. Select No if the applicant works for a company or organization.
  • Applicant Income : Enter the applicant's monthly income.
  • Coapplicant Income : Monthly income of co-applicant.
  • Loan Amount : Total loan amount requested.
  • Loan Term : Loan repayment duration in months (example: 360 months).
  • Credit History : Select Good if applicant has good credit history.
  • Property Area : Area where property is located (Rural / Semiurban / Urban).
